Elegant Transparent White Sequin Fabric

Add a touch of glamour to your crafting projects with this 1 yard long transparent white sequin fabric. Featuring high-density 3mm round sequins, it offers a dazzling sparkle that catches the light beautifully. The fabric is crafted on a smooth, stretchy polyester mesh backing, which provides a soft and flexible drape, making it perfect for creating elegant garments, trim work, and decorative accents.

Whether you're designing a romantic bridesmaid dress, a catchy Halloween costume, or a glamorous party ensemble, this fabric brings a luxurious finish to your creation. It's ideal for special occasion attire due to its eye-catching shine and versatility. The fabric is cut smoothly without stitching, so you may notice a slight sequin loss at the edges. To ensure the sequins stay secure, simply give the fabric a gentle shake before starting your project.

With customizable size and color options, you can easily match this fabric to your unique design ideas. Its 48-inch width and 1-yard length make it a great option for those who need ample material for their creative endeavors. Durable and stylish, this shining sequin fabric for sewing is perfect for anyone looking to elevate their crafts with a touch of elegance and sparkle.
